About Me

Dr. HUYNH A. D. NGUYEN (Member, IEEE) received the B.E. degree in mechatronics engineering from Can Tho University, Vietnam, in 2009, and the M.Sc. degree in mechatronics engineering from the University of Siegen, Germany, in 2015. He earned his Ph.D. degree from the Faculty of Engineering and Information Technology, University of Technology Sydney (UTS), Australia, in 2024. He has worked as a Lecturer and a Researcher with the Department of Automation Technology, Can Tho University, since 2010. He was the Vice Chair of the UTS IEEE Student Branch, since 2021. His research interests include IoT technology, data processing and analysis, machine learning, and deep learning.
